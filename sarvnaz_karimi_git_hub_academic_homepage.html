About

Dr Sarvnaz Karimi is a Principal Research Scientist at CSIRO Data61 with nearly two decades of experience in NLP and Information Retrieval. She leads and contributes to research across health, climate adaptation, and agriculture. Sarvnaz is active in the international research community (ACL leadership, ARR Editor-in-Chief) and co-supervises PhD students across Australian universities.

Research Interests

Natural Language Processing · Information Retrieval · Biomedical Text Mining · Digital Health · Social Media Analytics · NLP for Climate Science & Agriculture · Large Language Models for Science

Selected Projects

  • Boeing Advanced Production Systems (APS) — NLP/AI at scale for documentation and knowledge assist (2024–present).
  • Climate Services for Agriculture (CSA) — Feasibility of LLM-based question answering for farm advisors (2023–present).
  • Science Digital (Genomics) — NLP lead on information extraction & summarisation (2023–2025).
  • Medical Decision Support (Precision Health) — Led a $2.2M project with top-ranked TREC evaluation results (2018–2024).
  • Syndromic Surveillance — Using social media for infectious disease monitoring (2017–2022).
